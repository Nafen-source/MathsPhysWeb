Convex Integration

This is a method for finding highly irregular solutions to PDEs. It is largely useful in studying non-linear problems such as those found in fluid mechanics, geometry, and material science.
The rough idea is to first satisfy the boundary conditions before adding small oscillations to adjust the differential operator to be closer to the source term.
